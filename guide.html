Client Guidelines

Please read through the process and policies below before we begin working on your project. This ensures a smooth, professional, and fast editing experience for both of us.

01

Submit Your Files

Upload your raw video clips, audio, and any specific brand assets to a Google Drive folder and share the link with me. Please make sure the link access is set to "Anyone with the link".

02

The 40% Downpayment

Work will not begin until a 40% upfront downpayment is received. This secures your spot in my editing schedule and acts as a commitment fee. The remaining 60% is strictly due upon project completion.

03

Editing & Watermarked Review

I will edit your video based on the package you selected. Once finished, I will send you a heavily watermarked version for your review. You can check the flow, transitions, and timing.

04 Final Delivery

Once you are happy with the preview, you will clear the remaining 60% balance. After payment is confirmed, I will immediately send you the final, high-quality, unwatermarked HD video file.

Revisions & Changes

Your package includes a specific set of basic revisions (fixing errors, slight timing changes). Complete style changes, changing the background music after the edit is done, or sending "new clips" to replace old ones will count as a brand new edit or require a paid revision fee.
