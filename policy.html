Terms & Policies

To maintain a high standard of quality and mutual respect, all clients must agree to the following 10 binding conditions before the commencement of any video editing project.

01

Scope of Work & Packages

Services are strictly limited to the features outlined in your chosen package. Any requests for styling, effects, or tasks outside the boundaries of the selected tier will be billed separately as Add-Ons. Free extra work is not provided under any circumstances.

02

The 40% Downpayment Rule

No project will be scheduled or started without a mandatory 40% upfront downpayment. This secures your slot and acts as a commitment fee. The remaining 60% balance is strictly due upon the completion and approval of the project.

03

Watermarked Previews

Upon completion of the edit, you will receive a heavily watermarked preview file for review. The clean, unwatermarked, high-resolution final file will only be released after the final 60% payment has been successfully cleared.

04

Revisions & Alterations

Minor adjustments (e.g., text typos, slight timing tweaks) are included. However, complete style changes, swapping background music after the edit is done, or replacing original raw clips with "new" ones will be treated as a new project or incur a paid revision fee.

05

Client Responsibilities

Clients must provide all raw clips, audio, brand logos, and specific instructions before editing begins. Files must be organized and shared via a working Google Drive link. Editing delays caused by disorganized or missing files are the client's responsibility.

06

Strict Add-On Billing

Features such as custom subtitles/captions (+₹100), custom post thumbnails (+₹100), or rush same-day delivery (+₹100) are not included in base packages unless explicitly stated. You will be quoted and billed for these additions if requested.

07

Delivery Timelines

The estimated delivery timeline (e.g., 1-2 days) begins only after the 40% downpayment is received AND all necessary files/instructions have been successfully downloaded from your drive link.

08

Cancellations & Refunds

If you choose to cancel a project after editing has officially commenced, the 40% downpayment is non-refundable to compensate for the time and labor already invested into your raw files.

09

Project Abandonment

If a watermarked preview is sent and the client fails to respond, request revisions, or clear the final payment within 7 days, the project will be considered abandoned. The final files will be archived, and a reactivation fee may apply to retrieve them.

10

Portfolio Usage Rights

Unless a strict Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) is requested and agreed upon beforehand, Gaurav Edits reserves the right to use short clips or screenshots of the final edited video for portfolio and promotional purposes on social media.
